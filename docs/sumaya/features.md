# Features in Sumaya

Sumaya offers a wide range of features and activities. This page covers everything you can do in the game.

## Vehicles

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of the vehicle spawner or dealership -->
<!-- ![Vehicle Spawner](/images/sumaya/features/vehicle-spawner.png) -->

Vehicles help you get around Sumaya faster. Visit the car dealership to **rent** or **buy** a vehicle using Sumaya Ringgit (SR).

### Available Vehicles

| Vehicle | Brand | Type | Level Required | Rent | Buy |
|---------|-------|------|---------------|------|-----|
| Protan Wira | Protan | Civilian | 1 | — | — |
| Protan Preve | Protan | Civilian | 1 | SR 1,000 | SR 5,000 |
| Protan X50 | Protan | Civilian | 20 | SR 1,000 | SR 5,000 |

All vehicles are 4-door sedans developed by Protan, a fictional Malaysian car brand inspired by real Malaysian manufacturers.

### How to Get a Vehicle

1. Visit the **car dealership** in the city.
2. Interact with the dealership to open the **Vehicle Spawner GUI**.
3. Browse available vehicles — locked vehicles will show their level requirement.
4. Choose to **rent** (temporary) or **buy** (permanent) using SR.
5. Select your preferred **colour** for the vehicle.
6. Choose an available **spawn pad** to bring the vehicle into the world.

::: warning Level Requirements
Some vehicles require you to reach a specific level before they become available. Keep playing and progressing to unlock better vehicles!
:::

### Vehicle Customisation

- **Colour Selection:** Choose from a range of colours before spawning your vehicle.
- **Registration Plates:** Vehicles are assigned registration plates that can be looked up by POLIS using their tablet.

### Vehicle Physics

Sumaya uses the **A-Chassis** vehicle physics system, providing realistic driving mechanics including steering, acceleration, and braking.

## Fishing

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of fishing by the water -->
<!-- ![Fishing](/images/sumaya/features/fishing.png) -->

Fishing is one of the best ways to earn SR and experience the rich aquatic life of Malaysia.

### How to Fish

1. Head to the **fishing zone** near the water.
2. Pick up or equip the **Fishing Rod** tool.
3. Stand within the fishing zone boundary (maximum 80 studs from water).
4. **Click** to cast your line into the water.
5. Watch for a **shake indicator** when a fish bites.
6. React quickly to set the hook and catch the fish!

### Fish Species

All fish in Sumaya are real Malaysian freshwater species. Each catch comes with an **educational infographic** teaching you about the species.

| Fish | Scientific Name | Catch Chance | XP | SR |
|------|----------------|-------------|-----|-----|
| Baung | *Hemibagrus nemurus* | Common | 15 | 8 |
| Empurau | *Tor tambroides* | Common | 15 | 8 |
| Patin | *Pangasius* | Common | 15 | 8 |
| Pelian | *Hampala macrolepidota* | Common | 15 | 8 |
| Tapah | *Wallago attu* | Common | 15 | 8 |

### Malaysian Fish Facts

Each fish you catch triggers an **infographic popup** with real-world facts about the species:

- **Empurau** — Known as the "King of Freshwater Fish", found in Sarawak's rivers. Can sell for over RM1,000/kg in real life.
- **Patin** — The key ingredient in Pahang's iconic Tempoyak Ikan Patin, a spicy fermented durian curry.
- **Baung** — A staple of Malay cuisine, traditionally cooked in Gulai Tempoyak or grilled with sambal.
- **Tapah** — One of Malaysia's largest freshwater fish, growing over 2 metres. The city of Tapah in Perak is named after it.
- **Pelian** — A popular sport fish known for its aggressive strikes, found in clean, fast-flowing rivers.

### Selling Fish

After catching fish, head to the **Fish Store** in the fishing area to sell your catch and collect your SR rewards.

## Fast Food Job

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of the fast food restaurant interior -->
<!-- ![Fast Food](/images/sumaya/features/fast-food.png) -->

Work as a fast food crew member at the burger restaurant to earn SR. The job involves preparing and serving burgers to NPC customers.

### How It Works

1. Enter the **Burger Fast Food** restaurant and interact to start your shift.
2. NPC customers will queue up and place specific **burger orders**.
3. Follow the preparation steps to fulfil each order.

### Burger Preparation Steps

| Step | Action | Station |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Take a **frozen meat patty** from the freezer | Freezer |
| 2 | Place the patty on the **grill** and cook it | Grill Station |
| 3 | Place a **bottom bun** on the prep station | Ready Station |
| 4 | Add the **cooked patty** and requested **condiments** | Ready Station |
| 5 | Place the **top bun** to complete the burger | Ready Station |
| 6 | Click the **paper bag** station to package the meal | Container |
| 7 | **Deliver** the bag to the correct customer | Counter |

::: warning Don't Burn the Patty!
If you leave the patty on the grill for too long, it will become burnt and unusable. Keep an eye on the cooking time!
:::

### Getting Paid

When you **end your shift**, your completed orders are tallied up:

- Correctly completed orders count towards your pay.
- Incorrect or incomplete orders will not earn you SR.
- You'll receive a **Pay Slip** showing your earnings for the shift.

## Housing & Residence System

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of the housing placement UI -->
<!-- ![Housing](/images/sumaya/features/housing.png) -->

Sumaya features a full residence system where you can claim land and build your own home.

### Claiming a Plot

1. Open the **House Placement** menu via the topbar icon.
2. Navigate to the **Claim Plot** section.
3. Walk to an available plot in the residential area.
4. Select the plot to claim it as your own.

### House Templates

Choose from four house designs:

| Template | Style | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Kampung House 1 | Traditional | Classic Malay village (kampung) house |
| Kampung House 2 | Traditional | Variant of the kampung house design |
| Kampung House 3 | Traditional | Another kampung house variant |
| Modern Terrace 1 | Modern | Contemporary terrace house design |

### Furniture

Furnish your home with the following items:

- **Bookshelf** — A decorative bookcase
- **Single Bed** — A bed for sleeping
- **Dining Table** — A table for meals
- **Coffee Table** — A small living room table
- **Triple Chair** — A three-seater sofa
- **Single Chair** — A single seat
- **Mirror** — A wall-mounted mirror

### Build Mode

Use the **Build** tab to enter build mode, where you can:

- Place and move furniture around your house
- Rotate and position items precisely
- Access placement controls for fine-tuning

### House Management

Use the **Manage** tab to control:

- **Doors:** Lock or unlock your front door to control access.
- **Lights:** Turn lights on or off inside your home.

### House Customisation

Use the **Colour** tab to change the exterior colour of your house, choosing from a palette of available colours.

## Phone System

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of the in-game phone -->
<!-- ![Phone](/images/sumaya/features/phone.png) -->

Every player has access to an in-game smartphone, accessible via the Phone tool in the toolbar.

### Phone Features

- **Settings:** Manage your in-game preferences and controls.
- **Residence:** View information about your claimed plot and house.
- **Passive Mode:** Toggle passive mode to prevent player interactions (see below).
- **Wallpaper:** Customise your phone's wallpaper with different designs.
- **Notifications:** Receive alerts for paychecks, incidents, and other events.

The phone features a realistic design with a front camera, volume buttons, and a side button.

## Banking & ATMs

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of the ATM interface -->
<!-- ![ATM](/images/sumaya/features/atm.png) -->

Sumaya has five ATM machines spread across the city for managing your finances.

### ATM Functions

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Deposit** | Transfer cash from your wallet into your bank account |
| **Withdraw** | Take cash from your bank account into your wallet |
| **Transfer** | Send money directly to another player's account |

### Wallet vs Bank

- **Wallet (cash on hand):** Used for immediate transactions like shop purchases and donations. You start with SR 50.
- **Bank account:** Safer storage for your earnings. You start with SR 1,000. Access via ATMs only.

## Passive Mode

Toggle **Passive Mode** via your phone to prevent unwanted player interactions. When passive mode is active:

- Other players cannot interact with you in harmful ways.
- Useful when exploring, building, or working without interruptions.
- Can be toggled on and off at any time.

## Day/Night Cycle & Weather

Sumaya features dynamic environmental systems:

- **Day/Night Cycle:** The game transitions through realistic times of day, with the lighting handler managing sunrise, daytime, sunset, and nighttime lighting. Street lights and lamp posts turn on automatically at night.
- **Rain System:** Periodic rain effects with the RainManager system adding atmosphere and immersion to the world.
- **Atmosphere:** Visual effects including bloom, depth of field, sun rays, and colour correction enhance the Malaysian tropical setting.

## Parachute

A parachute system is available in Sumaya, allowing players to experience aerial views of the city and safely descend from high points.

## Crash System

Sumaya features a vehicle crash system:

- Vehicles can collide and cause crashes.
- Crashed players may receive injuries that require medical attention from Kesihatan team members.
- A **Crash Tutorial** is shown to new players explaining the system.
- Players can toggle crash notifications on or off via the **Crash Toggle** setting.

## Admin System

Sumaya uses an admin system for game moderation. Beta testers have access to additional admin commands through the Beta Tester Admin plugin. The admin system includes customisable settings, themes, and plugins.
