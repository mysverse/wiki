# Gameplay Features in Bandaraya

Bandaraya is a comprehensive Malaysian city roleplay experience packed with interconnected systems. Here are the key gameplay features.

## Currency & Economy

### Bandaraya Ringgit (BR)

- BR is the primary in-game currency used for purchasing items, vehicles, and services.
- Earn BR through jobs such as Pos Nasional (post office delivery), taxi driving, and team-based salaries.
- Income is paid periodically based on your active job and team.
- Transfer BR to other players via the in-game phone.

### Vastrix Banking

- Vastrix ATMs are located throughout the city.
- Deposit BR into your Vastrix account in increments of BR 1,000, BR 5,000, BR 10,000, or BR 100,000.
- Use your Vastrix account for secure savings and transactions.

### HazBank

- HazBank is a key financial landmark in the city.
- The bank can be the target of robbery events (see Criminal Activities below).

## Vehicles & Dealerships

### Vehicle Dealerships

Bandaraya features vehicle dealerships where you can rent or purchase vehicles using BR. Available vehicles include a wide selection of Malaysian and international brands:

**Malaysian Brands:**

- **Proton:** Saga, Saga 2022, Inspira, Persona, Preve, Waja, Exora, X50, X70
- **Perodua:** Myvi, Myvi 2020, Bezza, Ativa, Aruz

**International Brands:**

- **Honda:** Civic, Civic Type R, City
- **Toyota:** Camry, Hilux, Vios, Vellfire
- **Mitsubishi:** Outlander, Lancer Evo X
- **Others:** Ford Mustang, Volvo S90, Nissan R35 GT-R, BMW i7, Mercedes C300, Volvo XC90

**Motorcycles:**

- Honda EX5, Honda CBR1000RR

Each vehicle can be customised with different colour options at the dealership.

### Vehicle Mechanics

- Vehicles can break down and require a **Toolkit** (BR 100) to repair.
- A **Jerrycan** (BR 800) can be used to refuel vehicles.
- Towing services are available for disabled vehicles.
- Vehicles have realistic driving physics with headlights, signals, and collision systems.

### Law Enforcement Tools

- **Taser:** Non-lethal takedown tool for police.
- **Handcuffs:** Detain and arrest suspects.
- **Radar Gun:** Detect speeding vehicles.
- **Tablet:** Look up vehicle registration information by plate number.
- **Radio (Voxel):** Team communication system for POLIS and Bomba.

## Fire System

- **Fire Incidents:** Fires spawn dynamically around the city when Bomba team members are present.
- **Hydrant System:** Fire hydrants are placed throughout the city for connecting supply lines.
- **Firefighting Process:**
  1. Drive the Fire Rescue Tender (FRT) to the fire location.
  2. Connect the **Supply Line** from the FRT to a nearby hydrant.
  3. Use the **Attack Line** (fire hose) to extinguish the fire.
- **Equipment:** Bomba members have access to fire hoses, supply lines, and utility tools.

## Arrest & Legal System

### Arrest Mechanics

- Law enforcement officers can arrest players who commit offences.
- Arrested players are moved to the **Arrested** team and must wait out their sentence.
- A dedicated **ArrestedGUI** displays arrest information including the arresting officer, time to release, and reference number.

### Summons System

- Officers can issue **summons** (traffic/legal citations) to players.
- The **SummonsView** GUI allows players to view their summons details.

### Search & Frisk

- Law enforcement can conduct searches on players using the **Searchee** system.
- Players are prompted to consent to or refuse a search.

### Penal Code

- The game features a comprehensive penal code system with defined offences and penalties.

## Roleplay Features

### Animations

Access the animation menu via the on-screen controls to perform:

- Sit, Chilling, Injured, Surrender, Squat, Crossed Arms, Leaning, and Stop.

### Interactive Elements

- **Piano:** Playable pianos located in the city with a full keyboard GUI, transpose, and volume controls.
- **Elevator System:** Functional elevators in buildings.
- **Seating:** Sit in vehicles, chairs, and benches across the city.
- **Placement System:** Place furniture and objects in designated areas.

### Communication

- **In-game Phone:** Call taxis, message players, request emergency services, manage vehicles, and transfer BR.
- **Radio System:** Team-based radio communication for emergency services (POLIS, Bomba).
- **Typing Bubble:** A visual indicator appears above your character when you are typing.

### Weather & Environment

- **Day/Night Cycle:** Dynamic time cycle with changing lighting conditions.
- **Rain System:** Dynamic weather with rain effects.
- **City Sounds:** Ambient city soundscape.

## Criminal Activities

### Bank Robbery

- Players can attempt to rob HazBank for high-risk, high-reward gameplay.
- Law enforcement teams will respond to robbery alerts.

### Illegal Dealers

- **Hacker NPC:** Purchase a hacking device for BR 5,000.
- **Gun Dealer:** Purchase illegal firearms (requires Firearms Licence gamepass).

### Alert System

- Emergency alerts are broadcast to relevant teams when incidents occur (robberies, fires, emergencies).
- Alerts appear on the minimap for responding teams.

## Competitive Mode (HardPoint)

- A team-based competitive game mode where teams contest control points.
- Features a scoring system with Home and Away teams.
- Announcements and point tracking displayed via the HardPoint UI.

## Map & Navigation

### Minimap

- A dynamic minimap in the corner of the screen shows your position, nearby points of interest, and active events.
- Toggle and expand the minimap for a broader view.

### Full Map

- Access the full map via the topbar for a complete overview of the city.
- Zoom in/out and drag to navigate.
- Icons show vehicles, packages, reports, taxi requests, tow requests, alerts, and player positions.

### Location Labels

- The current street/area name is displayed on screen as you move through the city.
